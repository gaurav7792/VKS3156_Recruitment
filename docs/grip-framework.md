# GRIP — Governing Rules for Interalliance Partnership

**Operating Principles & Governance**

## Core Purpose of GRIP

GRIP exists to provide structure, stability, and long-term competitiveness for the state. Its primary functions are:

1. Establish and rotate Presidency in a fair and predictable manner
2. Manage state transfers and integration of incoming players/alliances
3. Allocate Forts and Strongholds across member alliances
4. Protect the state from rogue actors and internal destabilization

## Operating Philosophy

- The spirit of the rules + basic logic governs all cases.
- This is not early game — baseline competence is expected.
- No "bad faith" or "low level" arguments will be entertained.
- "What does FFA mean?"-type debates are irrelevant.

## Membership Framework

- Any alliance may apply for GRIP membership through formal declaration.
- **Provisional Membership** is granted upon meeting a defined SvS performance threshold (500M\* points) in the upcoming cycle.
- **Full Membership** is granted upon:
  - Maintaining the SvS point requirement
  - Demonstrating consistent adherence to GRIP rules and conduct
- State transfers, alliance entry/exit, or structural changes must be declared in advance and may impact:
  - Protection status
  - Structure eligibility
  - Presidency consideration

> \* May change with age of the server, alliance size, and composition.

## Rules of Engagement

### 1. Protected Play

GRIP alliances and their academies may not:

- Attack, scout, burn, or interfere with cities, banners, or normal resource activity outside of sanctioned events.

### 2. Fair Structure Competition

- Facilities remain FFA / open competition unless explicitly assigned. No duplicate holding.
- Forts and Strongholds must follow agreed ownership or rotation.
- The following are prohibited:
  - Reward theft
  - Outside stacking or roster manipulation
  - Capturing Forts / Strongholds for another alliance

The goal is fair access — not denial through technicalities. No malicious event manipulation. (One-offs happen — don't be a dick about it with an axe to grind.)

### 3. State Before Politics

During:

- SvS
- Sunfire
- Tundra Arms League
- Frostdragon
- Other designated state-priority events

All alliances act in alignment with the agreed state battle plan. Internal disputes are paused until event completion.

### 4. Code of Conduct

Harassment, hate speech, or personal insults in World Chat or DMs are strictly prohibited. Keep the rivalry focused on gameplay — repeated verbal abuse will result in punishments.

### 5. Clear Accountability

- Questionable violations are reviewed by uninvolved alliance leadership.
- Decisions are based on:
  - Screenshots
  - Battle reports
  - Verifiable evidence

**Penalty escalation:**

1. Warning + restitution
2. Loss of event/structure privileges
3. Temporary removal of GRIP protections
4. Retaliatory attacks/rallies with Presidential punishments
5. Invitation to leave GRIP

Repercussions will be swift and decisive.

## Enforcement Standard

- Rules are enforced based on intent, impact, and pattern of behavior.
- Exploiting loopholes is treated as a violation of the system itself.
- GRIP prioritizes fairness, clarity, and decisive action over endless debate.
