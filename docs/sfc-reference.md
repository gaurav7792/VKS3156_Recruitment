# Interstate Sunfire Castle Battle — Reference Guide

## Event Structure

6 alliances divided into 2 teams. Each team determines its own strategy. The intent is to simulate a real SvS battle and train people in new roles.

**Example split:** HWZ + WLD + MAD  vs  VKS + PHX + EVL

To keep the fight competitive, gather availability info ahead of time and split alliances and rally leaders accordingly.

**Current SFC rotation:** HWZ → WLD → VKS → PHX → EVL → MAD

## Game Formats

### Format 1 — Full FFA (5 hours)

| Element | Rule |
|---|---|
| Castle | Open all 5 hours. Any team or alliance may rally or attack at any time. |
| Turrets | FFA for all 5 hours. |
| Presidency | Awarded to the victor's alliance per agreed rotation. |

### Format 2 — Split FFA (2.5 + 2.5 hours)

| Phase | Rule |
|---|---|
| **Phase 1** — H 0:00–2:30 | FFA. Castle open, rallies permitted, turrets FFA. |
| **Phase 2** — H 2:30–5:00 | Incoming President sits in castle. No castle rallies. Turrets remain FFA. |

> **NOTE:** Turrets are FFA for the full 5 hours in both formats.

## Roles

### Rally / Counter-Rally Leaders

| Aspect | Detail |
|---|---|
| Duty | Time rallies at castle with other leaders. Coordinate via DC/VC to hit perfect times. |
| Counter-rally | Use Overwatch to open counter-rally so your march lands just after the enemy hit. |
| Strategy | Minimize other states' time in castle. Initiate a swap soon after the counter-rally lands. |

### Rally Managers

| Aspect | Detail |
|---|---|
| Duty | Ensure rallies are filled correctly and efficiently. |
| FC priority | Helios > FC5 > FC4 > FC3 > FC2 > FC1. Prioritize FC4/FC5 troops for all castle rallies. |
| Swaps | At 5s to swap, there should be no rally leaders in the garrison. Rally Managers and Rally Leads must coordinate to time this well. |

### Comms Team

| Aspect | Detail |
|---|---|
| Duty | Bridge between Discord and alliance chat. Relay calls in real time, both directions. |
| Relay | Communicate Rally Manager, Rally Leader, and Overwatch calls as given. |
| Coordination | Coordinate player movement between alliances when needed. |

### Overwatch

| Aspect | Detail |
|---|---|
| Duty | Must be on DC/VC. Provide overall lookout and infer enemy strategy. |
| Countdown | Identify enemy rally launches and count down counter-rallies. |
| Castle watch | Monitor overall strategy and provide insight as needed. |

## Battle Etiquette

- Join rallies after the first minute — designated joiners enter first.
- Do not complain if removed from a rally. Leaders are optimizing FC composition and heroes.
